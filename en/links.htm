If you are looking for someone to undertake the foley art or the mix of your film (documentary, T.V. movie, ...), a sound enginer to go shooting, a music publisher for your soundtrack, or if you are an adept of virtual reality (VR 360) — you should contact Virtuel Audio's team without waiting any longer, for no one can beat the welcoming, loyalty and professionalism they invest in their job to have it done with efficiency, meticulousness and in a joyful mood.
I owe them (among a lot more) to take charge for the publishing of some of my works.

The intellectual property and the copyright associated to my compositions are handled by the SACEM ; to learn more about this organisation, the easiest way might be by visiting its official website.

In order to guarantee complete control and to prevent this website from featuring annoying advertisings, I made it myself with only my dirty fingers, a free text editor (Notepad++, which I recommend) and precious advices from W3Schools.com. HTML, CSS, Javascript, XML, SQL, PHP, ASP, ... whatever your question is, the answer is there.

By the way, if I chose such a dark design, it is for the purpose of saving energy: electrical consumption is indeed at its climax in white pixels whereas it is null in black ones.

The nirvana of musicians in general, and of the self-taught ones in particular, is according to me the indispensable International Music Score Library Project, my exclusive provider for study material and the creditor to which I owe the shift in my career.
